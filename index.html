Bio

I received a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science from the University of St Andrews in 2015 where I was accepted into the accelerated program allowing me to skip the first year. Now I am a PhD student in the Distributed Robotics Laboratory at MIT under the supervision of Daniela Rus. I am currently working on ridesharing algorithms and machine learning for future urban mobility.
