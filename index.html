Alex Wallar


I am a student contractor in the Distributed Autonomous Systems Group at the Naval Research Laboratory conducting research in information theoretic surveillance for multi-agent systems. I received a First Class Honours degree in computer science from the University of St Andrews in Scotland. Starting in September 2015, I will be a PhD candidate in the Distributed Robotics Laboratory at MIT developing algorithms for autonomous surveillance and persistent monitoring.
